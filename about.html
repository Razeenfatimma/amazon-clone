About Amazon Clone

Welcome to our Amazon Clone, an e-commerce platform built to provide a seamless online shopping experience. This project is designed to showcase basic HTML, CSS, and responsive design techniques. Whether you're here to shop or explore, we aim to provide an enjoyable experience for all users.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a simple and user-friendly platform where users can easily browse, search, and purchase a wide range of products. The goal is to make online shopping convenient, fast, and secure.

What We Offer

We offer a variety of products, from electronics and books to clothing and home goods. The Amazon Clone allows users to add products to their cart and complete a purchase—all in a simple and intuitive interface.

Educational Project

This website is a personal project created for educational purposes. It is designed to demonstrate the basic functionality of an e-commerce site with features like product listings, a shopping cart, and a simple navigation bar.
